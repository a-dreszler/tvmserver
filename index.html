What kind of server is it?

TVM Flan's Mod is a reactivation of long gone and forgotten Minecraft server called TPG Flan's Mod. The gameplay is based on either fighting other players on FPS shooter arenas or building your own base on survival part of the server. In the survival map you can team up with other players, create your own guild, build your fortress and fight other players using firearms and even tanks. There are also technical mods included, so you can build your own nuclear reactor and many more interesting structures!
